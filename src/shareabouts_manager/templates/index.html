Keep Communities Informed & Engaged

Information about projects is difficult for residents to find and track.

Sharing timely, helpful information is challenging for busy planners.

Not everyone has time to attend a public meeting, but people want to have access to information about their community.

Uninformed participants have an unsatisfactory experience, even on the greatest projects. And unhappy people slow down the public process for everyone else.

Plan In A Box helps you create and publish an easy to navigate, informative web page about your project.
